SUDO Belt Philosophy

Grade (GU) Belt Color Philosophy
10th GU No Belt Complete beginner. Empty vessel ready to learn the way of SUDO.
9th GU White Symbol of purity and beginning. Absorbing the basic knowledge.
8th GU Gray Understanding discipline. The first steps from ignorance to awareness.
7th GU Yellow Hope and light. First confidence in techniques begins to show.
6th GU Orange Energy and movement. Strength grows rapidly, passion awakens.
5th GU Red Fire and danger. Power must be controlled with wisdom.
4th GU Green Growth and balance. Skills flourish, inner peace develops.
3rd GU Purple Spiritual depth. Wisdom begins to shine through action.
2nd GU Blue Vastness of knowledge. Calm mind and precise technique.
1st GU Brown Readiness for mastery. Strength, humility, and responsibility unite.
